# Slp Outcome Measures Adult Neuro

- Comprehensive assessment of language function in four core domains: spontaneous speech, auditory comprehension, repetition, and naming.
- Boston Diagnostic Aphasia Exam - 3rd Edition
- Comprehensive assessment of language and communication, providing profiles for classic aphasia syndromes.
- Profile analysis across fluency, comprehension, repetition, and naming clusters
- Goodglass, H., Kaplan, E., & Barresi, B. (2001). Boston Diagnostic Aphasia Examination (BDAE-3). Philadelphia, PA: Lippincott Williams & Wilkins.
- Cognitive Linguistic Quick Test - Plus
- Rapid cognitive screening tool for detection of mild cognitive impairment (MCI).
- Nasreddine, Z. S., et al. (2005). The Montreal Cognitive Assessment, MoCA: a brief screening tool for mild cognitive impairment. Journal of the American Geriatrics Society, 53(4), 695-699.
- Saint Louis University Mental Status
- Brief screening for dementia and mild cognitive impairment, with adjustments for education level.
- Tariq, S. H., et al. (2006). The Saint Louis University Mental Status (SLUMS) examination for detecting mild cognitive impairment and dementia. American Journal of Geriatric Psychiatry, 14(11), 900-910.
- Clinical tool for identifying cognitive symptoms and predicting functional outcomes.
- Mansbach, W. E., et al. (2012). The Brief Cognitive Assessment Tool: a new screening instrument for detecting mild cognitive impairment and dementia. Aging & Mental Health, 16(2), 173-181.
- Standardized measure of confrontational naming abilities, sensitive to word-finding deficits.
- Sum of spontaneous correct names and correct names after semantic cues
- Kaplan, E., et al. (2001). Boston Naming Test (BNT). Philadelphia: Lippincott Williams & Wilkins.
- Reading Comprehension Battery for Aphasia - 2nd Edition
- Systematic assessment of reading comprehension in adults with acquired aphasia.
- Evaluates functional language skills in realistic tasks like clock reading, checkbook balancing, and medication management.
- Percentage accuracy across 10 functional domains
- Baines, K. A., et al. (1999). Assessment of Language-Related Functional Activities (ALFA). Austin, TX: PRO-ED.
- Communication Activities of Daily Living - 3rd Edition
- Assesses functional communication skills in naturalistic scenarios (shopping, doctor appointments, social cues).
- Raw score converted to percentile and severity rating
- Holland, A. L., et al. (2018). Communication Activities of Daily Living (CADL-3). Austin, TX: PRO-ED.
