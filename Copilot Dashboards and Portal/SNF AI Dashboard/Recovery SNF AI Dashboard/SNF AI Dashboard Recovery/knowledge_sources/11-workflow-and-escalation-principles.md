# Workflow And Escalation Principles

- Use structured workflows for repeatable, safety-relevant tasks.
- Route low-priority issues through routine review channels.
- Route medium-priority issues to the responsible shift or department owner.
- Route high-priority issues to rapid-response or equivalent escalation paths.
- Route critical events through immediate emergency response channels.
- Preserve context across retries and manual continuation whenever possible.
- When automation fails during a safety-relevant task, fall back to a documented manual process and notify the responsible owner.
