# Project Charter And Clinical Governance

## Project Summary

- Project name: SNF Command Center
- Project type: healthcare intelligence, workflow automation, and clinical decision support augmentation
- Primary objective: improve patient safety, clinical coordination, documentation quality, and operational visibility without replacing clinical judgment

## Scope

- In scope:
  - clinical assistant behavior and orchestration
  - workflow automation and alert routing
  - real-time dashboards and semantic modeling
  - governance, validation, privacy, and audit readiness
- Out of scope:
  - autonomous diagnosis
  - unsupervised treatment recommendation activation
  - production release without clinical and compliance sign-off

## Stakeholders

- Executive sponsor
- Clinical sponsor
- Nursing director
- Therapy director
- Pharmacy or medication-safety owner
- Infection prevention owner
- Compliance and privacy owner
- BI and data platform owner
- Engineering owner

## Governance Structure

- Steering committee: approves roadmap, release readiness, and material risk decisions.
- Clinical governance group: approves workflows, safety rules, thresholds, and patient-facing language.
- Data governance group: approves definitions, source mappings, quality controls, and access patterns.
- Privacy and compliance review: approves disclosures, retention, audit, and consent handling.
- Technical release review: approves deployment, rollback, observability, and operational readiness.

## Approval Gates

1. Design approval
2. Clinical validation approval
3. Compliance and privacy approval
4. Technical release approval
5. Post-release review
