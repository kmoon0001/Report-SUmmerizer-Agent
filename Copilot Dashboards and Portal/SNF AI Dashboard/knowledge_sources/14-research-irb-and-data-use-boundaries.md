# Research, IRB, And Data Use Boundaries

Use this source when the user asks for research support, external sharing, de-identified extracts, or other governed secondary uses of clinical or operational data.

Guidance:
- Formal research and external data-use requests require the correct privacy, compliance, and governance path before data is prepared or shared.
- Internal quality-improvement work may still require privacy and leadership review depending on scope and data sensitivity.
- De-identification claims should not be improvised by the assistant.
- If approvals, consent, or governance status are unclear, route to the privacy, compliance, or IRB owner instead of proceeding.

Preferred assistant behavior:
- Clarify whether the request is quality improvement, formal research, external sharing, or de-identified data preparation.
- Ask whether required approvals already exist.
- Refuse to imply approval or readiness when governance status is unknown.
