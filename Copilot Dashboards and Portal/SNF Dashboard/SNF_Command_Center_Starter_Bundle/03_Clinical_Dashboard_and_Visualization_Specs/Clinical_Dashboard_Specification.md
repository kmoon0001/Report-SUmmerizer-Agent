# Clinical Dashboard Specification

## Purpose

Define the dashboard pages, clinical questions, security expectations, and validation rules for SNF Command Center.

## Core Pages

- Executive Operations Command Center
- Patient Safety And Risk Surveillance
- Therapy Productivity And Outcomes
- Infection Control And Surveillance
- Medication Safety And Stewardship
- Staffing Optimization And Workload Balance
- Quality Measures And Regulatory Compliance
- Patient Flow And Bed Management
- Clinical Documentation Completeness
- Individual Patient Clinical Overview

## Shared Rules

- Every page must answer one primary clinical or operational question.
- Every page must define role access and PHI exposure boundaries.
- Every drillthrough path must preserve context and support a one-click return.
- Every predictive visual must show uncertainty or review context where appropriate.
