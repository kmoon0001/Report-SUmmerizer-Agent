# Clinical Smart Narrative Specs

## Purpose

Define the tone, content boundaries, and validation rules for Smart Narrative outputs.

## Tone

- concise
- clinically respectful
- operationally useful
- non-diagnostic

## Required Content

- current state
- important trend or change
- likely drivers based on available data
- action owner or next review point when appropriate

## Disallowed Content

- diagnosis claims
- unsupported causal certainty
- fabricated missing facts
