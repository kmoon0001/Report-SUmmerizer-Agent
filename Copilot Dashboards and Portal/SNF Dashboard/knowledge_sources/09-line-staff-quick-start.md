# Line Staff Quick Start

## Nursing

- Use patient risk stratification for a quick safety review.
- Use medication safety review when there is a med concern or mismatch.
- Use infection control surveillance when you suspect a cluster or isolation gap.

## Therapy

- Use therapy pickup opportunity to find re-review or follow-up needs.
- Use documentation completeness to spot missing charting.
- Use patient risk stratification when safety or decline may affect treatment.

## Quality And Leadership

- Use quality and compliance review for measure, citation, or survey-readiness questions.
- Use documentation completeness for remediation tracking.
- Use staffing and workload review for operational strain visibility.
