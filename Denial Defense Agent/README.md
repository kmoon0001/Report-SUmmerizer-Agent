# 🛡️ Pacific Coast Denial Defense Agent
## Strategic Revenue & Compliance Safeguard

The **Denial Defense Agent** is the forensic sentinel of the Pacific Coast Therapy Hub. It protects the facility from Medicare RAC/MAC audits, clinical documentation denials, and professional standards misalignment by applying 7-Layer XAI forensic scoring to every high-risk note.

### 🚀 Core Modules
- **Denial Defense Worker**: The primary forensic auditor for daily clinical documentation.
- **Medicare Appeal Generator**: Produces high-fidelity medical necessity rebuttals grounded in CMS regulations and Jimmo standards.
- **Professional Standards Auditor**: Cross-references notes against APTA, AOTA, and ASHA disciplinary frameworks.

### 🎯 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Denial Rate Mitigation**: Target <5% ADR/Denial rate.
- **Documentation Velocity**: 15-second compliance scoring.
- **Appeal Success Rate**: 90%+ redetermination success through regulatory citations.

### 🛡️ Platinum Standards
- **Jimmo Compliance**: Universal application of the 'Maintenance Standard' for chronic/neuro populations.
- **Forensic Scoring**: Confidence-weighted SHAP/LIME logic for medical necessity determination.
- **Regulatory Grounding**: Direct citations from the Medicare Benefit Policy Manual (MBPM).
