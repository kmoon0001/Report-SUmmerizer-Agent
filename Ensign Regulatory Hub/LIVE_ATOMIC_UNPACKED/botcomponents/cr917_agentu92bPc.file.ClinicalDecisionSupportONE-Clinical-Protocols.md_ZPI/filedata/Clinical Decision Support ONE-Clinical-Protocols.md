# ONE Clinical Protocols Summary (Falls, Mobility, Pressure Ulcers, Pain, Infection)

This document summarizes the ONE Clinical protocols referenced by the SimpleLTC Clinical Strategy Copilot. It is a high-level framework, not a procedural instruction set.

## Falls Protocol

- Identify intrinsic and extrinsic risk factors
- Assess mobility, balance, and environmental hazards
- Implement interdisciplinary interventions (Therapy, Nursing, IDT)
- Monitor for changes in gait, strength, or cognition

## Mobility Protocol

- Assess functional mobility and transfer safety
- Identify barriers to safe ambulation
- Implement strengthening, balance, and endurance interventions
- Coordinate with Nursing for rounding and monitoring

## Pressure Ulcer Protocol

- Identify risk factors (immobility, nutrition, moisture)
- Implement repositioning and pressure relief strategies
- Coordinate with Nursing for skin checks and documentation
- Monitor for early signs of breakdown

## Pain Protocol

- Identify source and pattern of pain
- Implement non-pharmacologic interventions
- Coordinate with Nursing for monitoring and communication
- Document functional impact and response

## Infection Control Protocol

- Identify early signs of infection
- Reinforce hygiene, handwashing, and PPE use
- Coordinate with Nursing for monitoring and escalation
- Document functional changes related to infection
