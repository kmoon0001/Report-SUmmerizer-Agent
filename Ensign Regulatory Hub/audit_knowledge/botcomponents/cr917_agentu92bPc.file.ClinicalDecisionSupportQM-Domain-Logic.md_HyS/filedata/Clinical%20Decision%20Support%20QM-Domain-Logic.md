# QM Domain-Specific Logic (Falls, Mobility, Pressure Ulcers, Pain, Infection)

This document provides domain-specific logic used by the SimpleLTC Clinical Strategy Copilot. It is a high-level framework, not a procedural rule set.

## Falls QM Logic

- Identify residents with recent falls
- Identify functional or environmental contributors
- Map to ONE Clinical Falls protocol
- Identify therapy, nursing, and system drivers

## Mobility QM Logic

- Identify decline in mobility or transfer ability
- Identify missed skilled opportunities
- Map to ONE Clinical Mobility protocol
- Identify interdisciplinary drivers

## Pressure Ulcer QM Logic

- Identify new or worsening pressure injuries
- Identify immobility, nutrition, or moisture contributors
- Map to ONE Clinical Pressure Ulcer protocol
- Identify therapy and nursing drivers

## Pain QM Logic

- Identify residents with uncontrolled pain
- Identify functional impact of pain
- Map to ONE Clinical Pain protocol
- Identify interdisciplinary drivers

## Infection QM Logic

- Identify residents with recent infections
- Identify functional changes related to infection
- Map to ONE Clinical Infection protocol
- Identify interdisciplinary drivers
