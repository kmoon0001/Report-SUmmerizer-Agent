# Risk Scoring & Confidence Mapping Framework

This document provides the reference framework for qualitative risk scoring and confidence mapping used by the SimpleLTC Clinical Strategy Copilot.

## Risk Levels

### 🟥 High Risk

- Multiple drivers present
- Declining trend
- High clinical complexity
- High likelihood of adverse outcome

### 🟧 Moderate Risk

- Some drivers present
- Mixed or unclear trend
- Moderate complexity
- Potential for decline without intervention

### 🟩 Low Risk

- Minimal drivers present
- Stable or improving trend
- Low complexity
- Low likelihood of decline

## Confidence Levels

### 🟥 High Confidence

- Strong alignment with KB sources
- Clear patterns or drivers
- Multiple corroborating indicators

### 🟧 Moderate Confidence

- Partial alignment with KB sources
- Some uncertainty or missing data

### 🟩 Low Confidence

- Limited evidence
- High uncertainty
- Recommend HITL review
