# CMS/Jimmo Coverage Logic (Therapy Eligibility Framework)

This document summarizes the CMS and Jimmo coverage principles referenced by the SimpleLTC Clinical Strategy Copilot. It is a high-level framework, not a procedural rule set.

## Coverage Principles

- **Improvement is not required for coverage.**
- Skilled therapy may be covered to maintain function or prevent decline.
- Skilled therapy is appropriate when the complexity of the condition requires a therapist.
- Maintenance therapy may be provided by a therapist when clinical judgment and skill are required.

## Skilled Need Indicators

- Complexity requiring therapist-level decision-making
- High risk of decline without skilled intervention
- Need for therapist-level modification of plan
- Need for therapist-level assessment of response to treatment

## Documentation Principles

- Describe complexity, not just tasks
- Describe risk of decline
- Describe therapist-level reasoning
- Describe why a therapist—not a tech or aide—is required
