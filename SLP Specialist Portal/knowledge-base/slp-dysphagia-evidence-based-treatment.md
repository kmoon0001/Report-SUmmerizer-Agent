# Slp Dysphagia Evidence Based Treatment

- Severe cognitive impairment preventing participation
- Monitor O2 saturation continuously during FEES
- Coordinate with nursing for meal supervision
- Interventions for dysphagia in acute and subacute stroke (Geeganage 2022)
- Modified Barium Swallow Study interpretation and treatment planning protocol
- Cochrane Review, ASHA Practice Portal
- International Dysphagia Diet Standardisation Initiative framework implementation
