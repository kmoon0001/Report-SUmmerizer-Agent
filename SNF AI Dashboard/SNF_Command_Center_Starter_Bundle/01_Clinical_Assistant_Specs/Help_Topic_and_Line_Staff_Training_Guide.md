# Help Topic And Line Staff Training Guide

## Help Topic Purpose

Provide a simple, role-aware help entry point for staff who ask how to use the assistant or dashboard.

## Help Topic Behavior

- detect phrases such as `help`, `how do I use this`, `what can you do`, and `show examples`
- route to role-specific examples
- provide short answers and optional next-step buttons

## Quick Start Structure By Role

- What this tool helps with
- Example questions to ask
- What the top dashboard tiles mean
- What to do when something looks wrong
- When to escalate to a supervisor or clinician
