# Clinical Handoff Communication Protocol

## Standard

Use SBAR for shift report, escalation, transfer, and interdisciplinary communication.

## SBAR Structure

- Situation: what is happening now
- Background: what relevant context the next person needs
- Assessment: current clinical or operational concern
- Recommendation: what needs to happen next and by whom

## Safety Rules

- Highlight code status, allergy issues, active precautions, and recent deterioration first.
- Require explicit acknowledgment for critical handoff items.
- Route unresolved high-risk concerns to the licensed clinician or supervisor before closeout.
