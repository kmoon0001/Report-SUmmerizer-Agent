# Clinical Image Review Boundaries

Use image intake only when the image is approved for clinical use and the next step is clear.

Rules:

- Confirm consent, approved capture, and current policy before using a clinical image.
- Keep uploaded images clear, current, and under the supported platform size limit.
- Use images to support review, escalation, and workflow routing, not stand-alone diagnosis.
- Wound, medication, fall-scene, and safety images require human review before operational action.
- If the image is blurry, incomplete, or lacks context, say so and route to human review.
- Never claim certainty from an unvalidated image-review backend.
- Use the minimum necessary image and avoid unrelated patient or environmental detail when possible.
