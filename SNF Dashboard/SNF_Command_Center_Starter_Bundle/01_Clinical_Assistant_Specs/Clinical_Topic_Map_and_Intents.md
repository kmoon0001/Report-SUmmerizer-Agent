# Clinical Topic Map And Intents

## Primary Topics

- Patient risk stratification
- Therapy pickup opportunity
- Medication safety review
- Infection control surveillance
- Environmental safety assessment
- Care plan adherence and goal attainment
- Staffing adequacy and workload distribution
- Quality measure performance
- Regulatory compliance tracking
- Family communication and satisfaction tracking
- Rehabilitation discharge planning
- Advance care planning documentation

## Shared Intake Pattern

1. Identify role and purpose.
2. Identify patient, unit, or facility scope.
3. Ask for timeframe.
4. Route to the correct workflow or dashboard page.
5. Return concise results with action owner and timing.
