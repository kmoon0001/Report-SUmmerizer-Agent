# Clinical Accessibility And Usability Specs

## Minimum Requirements

- Support keyboard navigation.
- Support screen-reader-friendly labeling where the platform allows it.
- Use high-contrast color schemes for critical risk indicators.
- Avoid color-only encoding for urgent states.
- Validate bedside readability in low-light conditions.
- Validate touch targets for tablet and mobile use with gloved interaction where relevant.

## Testing Contexts

- Nursing station
- Therapy gym
- Executive office
- Bedside tablet
- Mobile phone
