# Clinical Key Influencers Specs

## Purpose

Define how Key Influencers visuals should be used in clinical and operational dashboards.

## Allowed Use

- identify likely drivers of pressure injuries, falls, readmissions, staffing pressure, and documentation gaps
- support exploration, not diagnosis or sole-cause claims

## Exclusions

- do not use unsupported demographic splits without governance review
- do not present a driver as a proven cause without supporting evidence

## Output Rules

- show the metric being explained
- show the candidate driver
- include context about sample size and confidence
- include review language when acting on the output may affect patient care
