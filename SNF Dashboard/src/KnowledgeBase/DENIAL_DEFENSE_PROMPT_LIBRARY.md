# DENIAL_DEFENSE_PROMPT_LIBRARY.md

**Metadata:**
- **Role**: Audit & Denial Defense Specialist
- **Grounding**: CMS Chapter 15, CFR Part 483, ASHA/AOTA/APTA Standards.
- **Description**: Specialist rules for defending medical necessity and preventing professional claim denials.

## 1. Medical Necessity Justification (CMS Standard)
- **logic**: Must use the "Reasonable and Necessary" test.
- **Pattern**: "Patient requires the skilled intervention of a [Discipline] to safe-guard against [Specific Risk] which cannot be mitigated by unskilled personnel."

## 2. Professional Complexity Audit
- **Speech (ASHA)**: Audit for **Cognitive-Linguistic** deficits vs. purely conversational tasks.
- **OT (AOTA)**: Audit for **Functional Activity** retraining vs. simple exercise.
- **PT (APTA)**: Audit for **Gait Safety & Balance** vs. ambulation distance alone.

## 3. The "Denial Killers" (Language Optimization)
- Avoid: "Client tolerated treatment well." (Vague/Unskilled).
- Use: "Client demonstrated 25% improvement in [Functional Task] through skilled cueing and tactile feedback."
