# SNF PT Documentation Standards

## Purpose
Reference for PT documentation in skilled nursing facilities. Use this source for required PT note elements, skilled intervention wording, measurable function, and medical necessity phrasing.

## Daily Note Essentials
- Mobility tasks addressed
- Gait, transfers, balance, strengthening, endurance, or neuromuscular interventions performed
- Assist level and cueing required
- Measurable performance such as distance, repetitions, time, tolerance, or device use
- Response to treatment and limiting factors
- Why skilled PT was required
- Plan for the next visit

## Skilled PT Language
- Emphasize therapist analysis, progression, safety monitoring, cueing, and modification of task demands.
- Describe functional impact, not exercise performance alone.
- Connect treatment to deficits in gait, transfers, bed mobility, standing balance, endurance, or fall risk.

## Measurable PT Examples
- Ambulated 75 ft with RW and CGA with verbal cues for step length and posture.
- Completed sit-to-stand transfer training x5 reps with Min A and sequencing cues.
- Tolerated standing balance activity for 3 minutes with intermittent UE support and SBA.

## Avoid
- Vague statements such as tolerated therapy well without functional detail.
- Listing exercises only without skilled rationale.
- Missing assist level, cueing, safety concerns, or clinical response.
