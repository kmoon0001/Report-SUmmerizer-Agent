# SNF ST Documentation Standards

## Purpose
Reference for ST documentation in skilled nursing facilities. Use this source for swallowing, cognition, communication, memory, attention, language, voice, and caregiver education guidance.

## Daily Note Essentials
- Swallowing, cognitive-communication, language, memory, attention, problem solving, orientation, voice, speech intelligibility, or caregiver education addressed
- Cueing or assist level required
- Diet texture or liquid consistency if relevant
- Measurable response or performance
- Why skilled ST was required
- Recommendations and plan

## Skilled ST Language
- Emphasize therapist analysis, cueing hierarchy, compensatory strategy training, swallow safety, cognitive strategy implementation, and caregiver instruction.
- Link therapy to safe intake, communication effectiveness, orientation, recall, problem solving, and reduced care burden.

## Measurable ST Examples
- Demonstrated recall of 3/4 swallowing precautions after spaced retrieval with Min verbal cues.
- Completed orientation task with 80% accuracy given visual aid and moderate verbal cueing.
- Consumed mechanical soft solids with no overt s/s aspiration using trained pacing strategy and supervision.

## Avoid
- Unsupported swallowing safety statements.
- Missing diet or liquid details when dysphagia is addressed.
- Vague cognitive wording without task, accuracy, or cueing.
