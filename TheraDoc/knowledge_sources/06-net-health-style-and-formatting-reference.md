# Net Health Style And Formatting Reference

## Purpose
Formatting guidance for concise narrative notes compatible with Net Health / Rehab Optima-style workflows.

## Preferred Style
- Concise, direct, skilled narrative
- Light section labels only when useful
- Minimal boilerplate
- Functional and measurable wording

## Recommended Narrative Flow
- Subjective or context
- Objective skilled interventions
- Measurable patient performance and response
- Assessment and clinical reasoning
- Plan

## Formatting Guidance
- Use approved abbreviations consistently.
- Keep note length efficient for EHR entry.
- Place measurable data near the related intervention.
- Keep the plan specific and clinically relevant.
