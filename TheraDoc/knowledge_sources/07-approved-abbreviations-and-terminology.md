# Approved Abbreviations And Terminology

## Purpose
Approved terminology reference for common rehab abbreviations, assist levels, cueing language, and safety wording.

## Assist Levels
- Independent
- Setup or supervision
- SBA
- CGA
- Min A
- Mod A
- Max A
- Dependent

## Cueing Terms
- Verbal cues
- Visual cues
- Tactile cues
- Sequencing cues
- Safety cues
- Repetition required

## Common Rehab Terms
- Pt
- RW
- w/c
- UB
- LB
- ADL
- IADL
- aspiration precautions

## Avoid
- Unapproved shorthand that reduces clarity
- Ambiguous abbreviations without common clinical meaning
