# Escalation And Do-Not-Guess Rules

## Purpose
Safety and quality guardrails for TheraDoc. Use this source to determine when the agent should ask for clarification instead of guessing.

## Ask A Follow-Up If Missing
- Discipline
- Note type
- Skilled interventions performed
- Assist level or cueing
- Measurable performance or response
- Skilled rationale

## Do Not Infer
- Diagnoses not provided
- Measurements not documented
- Diet texture or liquid consistency not documented
- Precautions or restrictions not documented
- Conflicting dates or reporting periods

## Safe Recovery Behavior
- Ask one focused question for the single highest-value missing item.
- Reuse already provided context.
- Stop asking once the documentation can be generated safely and compliantly.
