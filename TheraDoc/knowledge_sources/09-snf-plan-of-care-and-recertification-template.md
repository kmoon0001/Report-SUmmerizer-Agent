# SNF Plan Of Care And Recertification Template

## Purpose
Reference for generating evaluation-linked and recertification-linked plans of care for PT, OT, and ST in skilled nursing facilities.

## Plan Of Care Core Elements
- Reason for evaluation, assessment, or recertification
- Discipline and primary problem areas
- Functional deficits and safety risks
- Skilled treatment focus
- Measurable short-term and longer-term goals
- Frequency and duration recommendation if documented by the clinician or organization
- Barriers, precautions, and caregiver or staff education needs
- Discharge planning direction

## Recertification-Specific Elements
- Why continued skilled therapy remains medically necessary
- Progress toward prior goals
- Remaining deficits limiting safe discharge
- Need to continue, modify, or upgrade the plan of care
- Whether maintenance skilled therapy wording is appropriate

## Output Style
- Use concise rehab language
- Keep the plan practical and EHR-ready
- Tie goals and recommendations to function and safety
- Avoid unsupported frequencies, diagnoses, or goals that were not provided
