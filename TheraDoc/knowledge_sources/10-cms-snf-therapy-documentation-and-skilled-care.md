# CMS SNF Therapy Documentation And Skilled Care

## Purpose
Primary CMS reference for SNF therapy documentation, skilled care expectations, and medical-necessity framing.

## Use This Source For
- Skilled-care criteria
- Therapy documentation expectations in SNF settings
- Medical necessity and functional justification language
- Progress, discharge, and plan-of-care reasoning

## Key Guidance
- Show why the service required therapist skill rather than routine repetition.
- Tie treatment to measurable function, safety, monitoring, cueing, progression, or modification.
- Keep documentation grounded in patient-specific deficits, response, and ongoing need.
- Avoid boilerplate language that fails to explain skilled need.

## Best Used With
- SNF discipline-specific documentation standards
- Medicare Benefit Policy Manual Chapter 15
- SNF Services MLN documentation guidance
