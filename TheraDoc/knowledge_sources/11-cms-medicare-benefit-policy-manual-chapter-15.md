# CMS Medicare Benefit Policy Manual Chapter 15

## Purpose
Anchor CMS source for therapy coverage, documentation logic, and skilled-therapy expectations.

## Use This Source For
- Coverage and payment logic for therapy services
- Therapy documentation structure and support requirements
- Plan of care, progress, and discharge justification
- Skilled service rationale when documenting therapy episodes

## Key Guidance
- Tie services to a clearly documented need for therapist skill.
- Keep the plan of care and progress logic aligned with the documented functional deficit.
- Use objective evidence of change, response, or continued limitation.
- Avoid unsupported assumptions about service necessity or improvement.

## Best Used With
- CMS SNF therapy documentation guidance
- SNF discipline-specific documentation standards
- Skilled necessity and compliance guide
