# AOTA Documentation Of OT Services

## Purpose
OT documentation reference for functional, participation-focused, and skilled occupational therapy language.

## Use This Source For
- OT daily, progress, evaluation, recertification, and discharge documentation
- ADL and IADL reasoning
- UE function, coordination, cognition, safety, and adaptive equipment language
- Occupational performance and participation framing

## Key Guidance
- Document how OT affects function, routine participation, and safety.
- Include the occupation-based reason for intervention, not just the task.
- Capture assist level, cueing, and the impact of compensatory strategies or adaptive equipment.
- Link skilled OT to problem solving, grading, training, or safety monitoring.

## Best Used With
- SNF OT documentation standards
- Skilled necessity and compliance guide
- Plan of care and recertification template
